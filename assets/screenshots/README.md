# Grahak AI Screenshot Repository

This folder is designed to host screenshots demonstrating the visual capabilities, metrics dashboards, and user workspaces of the Grahak AI application. 

Please capture the following screenshots from the running Streamlit app and save them into this folder:

---

## 1. Dashboard Home
* **Filename:** `dashboard_home.png`
* **Capture Location:** Launch `streamlit_app.py`, open the local URL, and capture the header area showing the brand logo (`Grahak AI`), status badges, **Revenue Protected** hero card (with the recovery rate badge), and the action buttons.

---

## 2. Revenue Protection Workspace
* **Filename:** `revenue_protection_workspace.png`
* **Capture Location:** Scroll down to the **Customer Selection** and **Retention Workspace** sections. Capture the dropdown select widget, the churn score gauge/metrics (Tenure, Spend Trend, Monthly Charges), and the corresponding risk badges (High, Medium, Low Risk).

---

## 3. Recovery Campaign Generator
* **Filename:** `recovery_campaign_generator.png`
* **Capture Location:** Look at the **Action Campaign Generator** section. Select a customer requiring a campaign (e.g. `8451-BYRNY` or `3668-QJUXO`). Capture the generated campaign type card, the recovery probability percentage, and the fully rendered **Personalized Customer Success Email Template** textarea.

---

## 4. Revenue Planner
* **Filename:** `revenue_planner.png`
* **Capture Location:** Go to the chart layout sections. Capture the Plotly Express bar charts mapping churn scores and risk distributions, showing how risk points are partitioned.

---

## 5. Business Impact Summary
* **Filename:** `business_impact_summary.png`
* **Capture Location:** Turn on **Executive Presentation Mode** in the sidebar. Navigate to **Slide 5 (Business Impact)** or scroll to the bottom metrics list. Capture the timeline steps indicating identification, prioritization, strategy, and protected revenue.
