# Grahak AI: Business Impact Analysis & Executive Summary

This document details the financial implications, retention campaign efficiencies, and strategic business insights derived from the Grahak AI pilot implementation.

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Grahak AI is a predictive customer retention system designed to defend subscription revenue. By combining automated churn risk scoring with Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) forecasting, Grahak AI moves the business from a reactive stance (responding to cancellations) to a proactive strategy (preventative campaigns). 

In our initial cohort pilot of high-risk accounts, the platform successfully identified financial vulnerabilities, prioritized key accounts, and automatically generated retention workflows—resulting in an expected revenue recovery of **₹194.69 Lakh** from a total exposure of **₹648.96 Lakh**.

## 4. Business Insights

### A. The Cost of Churn vs. Retention
Our analytics indicate that active customer lifetime value averages **$639.78** (scaled metric), whereas churned accounts average only **$54.08**. This difference proves that early retention interventions yield a high Return on Investment (ROI) compared to the cost of customer acquisition.

### B. Risk-Adjusted Campaign Management
By reserving premium incentives (such as the 20% Premium Discount) for customers with high projected lifetime value, Grahak AI prevents margin erosion. Standard support or lower-tier incentives (Loyalty Points, Feedback Credits) are matched with lower-value or medium-risk accounts.

### C. Proactive Strategic Planning
Segmenting customers by tenure shows that **New (0-1 year)** customer cohorts account for the highest concentration of churn. The company can leverage this insight to redesign onboarding sequences, targeting customer success resources during the critical first 12 months.
